Title: Software Developer

Key Responsibilities:  Assist in the definition of requirements and specifications.  Design and develop software solutions in .NET and SQL to a very high standard  Planning and performing unit and regression testing  Problem solving and technical support (2nd line)  Working closely with business partners and endusers. Any experience of serial /IP communications, ATC or Meteorological systems would be a distinct advantage. Salary dependent on skill and experience.
