Title: Embedded Software Engineer
Full Description: MCS Group is working with one of the most exciting technology firms in Northern Ireland on a role suited to an experienced software engineer with expertise in embedded software / firmware development. Our client is set to become a leader in its field through the development of high performance hardware and integrated software solutions, designed to service the medical / healthcare sectors. Working in a small but growing team you will be involved in all stages of the development lifecycle as well as assisting the engineering team with product development, research and testing. You will be developing and delivering highly robust, highly reliable high performance software for specialist devices. Specifically some of the core responsibilities will include:  Assisting in analysis of user requirements  Developing specifications and system design  Performing software research and programming tasks as required  Assisting with ongoing analysis and testing of solutions to ensure optimal quality including performance testing, unit testing, diagnosis and troubleshooting of problem applications.  Responding promptly and professionally to bug reports and support requests  Contributing to product documentation and training manuals You will be successful in this position if you are experienced in C / C++ / C and have a good level of proficiency and passion for embedded software and firmware development. Ideally you will proven ability in developing and debugging embedded software and firmware spanning multiple processor cores and subsystems in realtime multitasking, multithreaded environments. You should be familiar with reading schematics, debugging hardware, and have proficiency with equipment such as logic analyzers, oscilloscopes, JTAGs, and other lab and test equipment. This is an excellent opportunity to join a talented team at an exciting stage of the company s development where you will be a core part of it. You will be rewarded with a generous salary and a flexible working environment.
