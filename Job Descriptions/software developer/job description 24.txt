About the job
Mailercloud, an innovative information technology and services company, is seeking a talented Software Developer to join our team. As the Software Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining software solutions that meet the needs of our customers and improve their experience with our products. You will work in a collaborative environment where your ideas are valued and your skills are appreciated.

Responsibilities
Design, develop, and maintain software applications and features
Write and execute unit tests to ensure the software meets quality standards
Collaborate with other developers to design and implement new features and enhancements
Participate in code reviews to ensure code is efficient, maintainable, and scalable
Troubleshoot and debug software issues
Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in software development
Provide support to customers and internal teams with technical issues and inquiries

Requirements
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Go and PHP
Experience with software development methodologies such as Agile and Scrum
Familiarity with database technologies such as SQL and NoSQL
Knowledge of web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Understanding of object-oriented programming concepts
Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
