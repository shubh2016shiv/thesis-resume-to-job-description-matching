Title: Quantitative Developer
Full Description: Exposed solutions are recruiting for their London based client who currently has an exciting opportunity for a Quantitative Developer to join their team. They are working with a large database of domestic energy readings, developing algorithms to derive consumer and utility insight that can be deployed at scale. This includes appliance disaggregation, personalised energy usage, tariff design and other business insight. The successful individual will join the existing team to work on developing algorithms to extract insight from smart metering and other energy data. These algorithms are designed to work at scale, and are incorporated in utility insight systems that server millions of customers. Our clients' quantitative developers work with data scientists to deliver market leading insight for the energy sector Main duties and responsibilities: The role requires high level mathematical analysis skills to identify short and long term characteristics and trends from the data, identifying what energy is being used for and how this relates to behavioural patterns.
