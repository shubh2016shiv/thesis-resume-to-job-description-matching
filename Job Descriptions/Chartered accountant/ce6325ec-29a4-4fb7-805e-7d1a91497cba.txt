Title: Group Management Accountant
Full Description: Sharp Consultancy is currently recruiting on behalf of one of the most reputable businesses in the Sheffield area for a Group Management Accountant. You will report directly into the Group Financial Controller and you will be responsible for the management accounting within the group as well as managing a team of two part qualified accountants. Your responsibilities will include: management of the monthly accounts consolidation process and the production of statutory accounts, ensuring the financial accounting records are maintained accurately and correctly at all times, significant project exposure including MI and Cost analysis, liaise with the internal auditors to ensure that financial best practice is adopted across the group, ensure compliance within internal and external controls, full responsibility for the UK VAT returns. All applicants must be chartered accountants having trained and qualified within a top 10 firm. Experience of a large ERP system and Advanced Excel skills would be highly advantageous but not essential.
