Title: Financial Controller
Full Description: This fast growing SME business with multiple divisions is looking for a recently qualified ACA from practice to make a move out into industry. The role will work very closely with the Group FD and take ownership and responsibility for the full cycle of accounting for a small but growing division. We are looking for a handson and ambitious Chartered Accountant with IFRS experience to take financial control of the current business. You will also work on the due diligence for new acquisitions before integrating the acquired finance functions into a shared facility in West Yorkshire. The business is aiming for an AIM floatation in the coming years and has a senior Executive Board with a long history of success in this field. Applicants need to have a keen interest in entrepreneurial business. You will be expected to develop hands on experience of every part of the financial process and use this to develop rapport and efficiency within your team as it grows. Long term opportunities are significant with substantial plans for growth within this niche and highly profitable organisation. Candidates must be eligible to work in the UK full time without restriction. To apply please send your CV to the email address stated, quoting our reference and specifying which website you saw this position advertised on. Due to the high volume of applications we receive it is not always possible to respond to unsuccessful applicants. Therefore if we have not responded to your application within seven days please note that on this occasion your application has not been successful.
