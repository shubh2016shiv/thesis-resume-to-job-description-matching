Title: Bookkeeper
Full Description: A firm of Chartered Accountants in Manchester city centre is looking to recruit an experienced Bookkeeper. As a Bookkeeper within this firm, you will be responsible for all aspects of bookkeeping for a number of clients, and acting as a sole Bookkeeper for a high profile business. You will be responsible for raising sales invoices, purchase orders, banking, payment runs, bank reconciliations and management accounts work. In order to apply for this role, you will have strong bookkeeping experience and ideally experience of Sage. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills are essential. In return, you will be offered a very competitive salary and benefits package working in a modern, yet friendly working environment.
