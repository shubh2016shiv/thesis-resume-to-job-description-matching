About the job
Key Responsibilities
Craft and implement the companys comprehensive financial strategy, encompassing capital structure, budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning.
Oversee mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures, including due diligence, deal structuring, and negotiation processes.
Establish rigorous risk management practices to identify, assess, and mitigate risks impacting the companys financial performance.
Direct capital allocation, ensuring effective utilization of resources to achieve the companys strategic objectives.
Drive cost management initiatives to enhance profitability and maintain a competitive position in the market.
Design and monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) to evaluate the companys financial performance and progress towards strategic goals.
Ensure efficient cash management, maintaining adequate cash flow to meet operational needs, service debt obligations, and make strategic investments.
Lead investor relations efforts, effectively communicating the companys financial performance, strategy, and outlook to shareholders, analysts, and other stakeholders.
Oversee tax planning and compliance, adhering to relevant tax laws and minimizing tax liabilities.
Ensure compliance with financial regulations and reporting requirements, including those set by the SEBI, stock exchange(s), and (latest) accounting standards.
Attract, develop, and retain a high-performing finance team while cultivating a robust financial culture within the organization.

Chartered Accountant.

Profound knowledge of financial reporting standards (e.g., Ind-AS), securities laws, and stock exchange regulations.
Demonstrated experience in managing investor relations, equity and debt financing, and M&A transactions.
Strong expertise in risk management, corporate governance, and financial controls.
Exceptional communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to forge strong relationships with stakeholders at various levels.
Unwavering commitment to ethical leadership and maintaining the highest standards of integrity.
Adaptability and dedication to continuous learning in a rapidly evolving business landscape.
