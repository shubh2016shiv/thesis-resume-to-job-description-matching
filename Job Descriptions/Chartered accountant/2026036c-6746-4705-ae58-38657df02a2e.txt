Title: Corporate Finance Executive/Manager
Full Description: A rare opportunity to join an instantly recognisable national corporate finance house in Leeds. Working within the lead advisory team, you will advise on all aspects or corporate finance including mergers, acquisitions, disposals, MBO's and MBI's and fund raising. Current transactional advisory experience is essential although this could be within corporate finance, transaction services or corporate recovery/restructuring. You should be a qualified chartered accountant (ACA or CA) with an impeccable pass record and academic grades, working within a major firm of accountants. In addition, business development skills and an existing network of contacts amongst Yorkshire based intermediaries would be advantageous. A clear promotional path is laid out with a highly competitive reward package. Manager grade offers the higher of the salary band plus a car allowance. Candidates must be eligible to work in the UK full time without restriction.
