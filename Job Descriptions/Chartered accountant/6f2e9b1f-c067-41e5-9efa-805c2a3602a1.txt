Title: Client Portfolio Manager
Full Description: Due to continued growth of the their client base, a leading firm of Chartered accountants are looking to recruit an experience Client Portfolio manager for their offices in Sheffield The business boasts an exceptional and varied client base, spanning across many business sectors, which includes a specialist focus towards media and entertainments industries. Working closely with the Partner, you will take responsibility for the management of a client portfolio, providing advice within financial and management accounting, tax planning, preparation of business plans and bespoke tax advice. You will also take the responsibility for the day to day client management, developing and forging long term relationships, managing work in progress and conducting client meetings an reviews. The successful individual will have already gained experience with the management of your own client portfolio from within a small to medium sized firm of accountants; you will have experience within personal taxation, financial and management accounting, corporate tax and tax planning, along with the ability of identifying new business opportunities. Due to the nature of the client base which will have a weighting towards property and entertainment clients, travel to the firms London office will be required, initially on a **** day/month basis. It is envisaged that the individual will be ACA or ACCA qualified, however individuals who are qualified by experience will also be considered. Randstad Financial Professional encourage applications from individuals of all ages backgrounds. Appointment will be made on merit alone but candidates must be able to demonstrate their ability to work in the UK.
