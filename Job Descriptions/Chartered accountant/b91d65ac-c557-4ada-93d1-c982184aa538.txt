Title: Business Analyst, Investment Banking  ACA sought
Full Description: Our client is a tier one investment bank, based in London, and with global operations. They are now looking for a Business Analyst, who will work within the business planning team of the IBD team. The role, working for the Head of Business Planning, will work on business analysis and strategic planning for the development of the IBD unit. This will look at the overall development of the business; competitor analysis; analysis of risk and strategy, analysis of team performance; financial planning and analysis. Its a great firrst move into investment banking for a commercial chartered accountant from one of the major accounting firms. You will have a really good understanding of investment banking and financial services clients and have a very good overview of the banking sector overall. Ideally, you will work in corporate finance, transaction services, restructuring or similar but we will also look at a great applicant from audit.
