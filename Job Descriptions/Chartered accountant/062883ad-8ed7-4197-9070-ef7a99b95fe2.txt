Title: Financial Accountant
Full Description: Alexander Daniels are currently recruiting a Financial Accountant for an international business with offices based in the West Midlands. This leading services business are growing quickly and as a result need an additional financial accountant to assist with group reporting and consolidations. The ideal candidate will be a chartered accountant with experience working in the group function of a multinational organisation. You will have experience of SAP and Hyperion. Advanced Excel skills are also required for this role. This role is to start in January 2012.
