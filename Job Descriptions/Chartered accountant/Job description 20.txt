Accounts & Audit Semi Senior / Accounts & Audit Senior- Droitwich

Accountancy Practice recruitment specialists Clark Wood are currently working with a well-respected local firm of chartered accountants in Droitwich, Worcestershire, who are now looking to recruit an Accounts & Audit Semi Senior or Accounts & Audit Senior.

This is a great opportunity to work as part of a successful team where you will assist with the completion of audit assignments, as well as having responsibility for the preparation of year-end accounts and the associated tax work for limited companies, sole traders, and partnerships.

The successful candidate will be rewarded with a highly competitive salary as well as study support to complete the ACA / ACCA qualification.
