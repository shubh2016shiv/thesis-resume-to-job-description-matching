Title: Financial Reporting Analyst
Full Description: Financial Reporting Analyst required for an international FTSE listed leisure business based in Central London. In this key role you will be responsible for the reporting and analysis for a number of International regions. This will involve preparing monthly analysis, explaining key trends and variances which will be presented to Senior Directors and the PLC board. You will also take a key role in preparing the group consolidation for the monthly reporting, budgeting and forecasting. You will be a qualified chartered accountant who has a strong technical background and has the ability to consolidate and present Management Information to the senior directorate.
