Title: Insolvency Administrator, Witney, ****
Full Description: A local firm of Chartered Accountants is looking to recruit an Insolvency Administrator to work closely with one of the partners. The ideal candidate will have previous demonstrable insolvency experience in a similar role, dealing with insolvency procedures.Key responsibilities will include:  To support the case management team in project managing a portfolio of cases, i.e. compliance, financial management, and case progression.  To assist with the preparation of a strategy and ensure matters are followedup in accordance with policy and the policy agreed for the case.  To provide support to the case managers and insolvency practitioners across the portfolio.  To draft all correspondence and reports to a high standard.  To prepare statutory reports and ensure compliance with statutory timescales.  To be risk aware and highlight issues to case manager.  To prepare the necessary financial information in relation to an assignment, e.g. receipts and payments, estimated outcomes, cashflow, dividend schedules, time costs, etc  To validate income and expenditure and prepare the necessary receipts and payments vouchers.  To prepare case billing.
