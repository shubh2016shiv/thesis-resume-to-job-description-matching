Title: Financial Controller
Full Description: Finance Manager (East Croydon)  Qualified ACA or ACCA. Excellent opportunity for a qualified accountant to join this expanding service based company. Reporting to the Managing Director, this role is responsible for:  Managing a team of three staff overseeing the day to day running of the finance function  Undertake the monthly management accounting  Oversee the year end accounting, and tax  Provide commercial support to the Board In many respects this is a very traditional Finance Manager role but represents an excellent opportunity for an individual keen to progress within an ambitious company within a growing market sector. Applicants must be:  ACA or ACCA qualified, possibly having trained within a firm of chartered accountants  Experienced in supervising staff  Able to draw upon both management accounting and financial accounting experience Our client is serviced by excellent transport links but there is also a designated car parking space if required.
