Title: Transaction Services Assistant Director
Full Description: Are you working within Transaction Services but with limited promotional prospects? If so, this is an excellent opportunity to join one of the region’s and country’s highest performing transaction services teams. As an Assistant Director within this international firm of chartered accountants, you will perform financial due diligence on the full range of corporate finance transactions. With such a senior grade, you must have considerable financial due diligence experience as an advisor from another international firm of chartered accountants or very similar environment. In addition, you should be a qualified chartered accountant, ACA or CA with excellent customer relationship, leadership and work winning/business development skills. In return you will advise on some of the region’s highest profile transactions and work with an exceptional team. Candidates must be eligible to work in the UK full time without restriction. To apply please send your CV to the email address stated, quoting our reference and specifying which website you saw this position advertised on. Due to the high volume of applications we receive it is not always possible to respond to unsuccessful applicants. Therefore if we have not responded to your application within seven days please note that on this occasion your application has not been successful.
