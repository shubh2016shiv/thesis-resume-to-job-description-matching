Title: Senior Financial Analyst
Full Description: Major consumer media business has created the new position of Regional Financial Analyst. Reporting to the Finance Director and with responsibility for an international region the role includes: support budgeting, forecasting long range/short term planning; work on new business cases i.e. evaluation of new channel launches; adhoc projects; working closely with commercial country heads; providing insightful analysis across products, channels and territories. A charismatic and highly intelligent qualified accountant, you will be either, a financial analyst within industry, or a chartered accountant looking to make your first move into industry.
