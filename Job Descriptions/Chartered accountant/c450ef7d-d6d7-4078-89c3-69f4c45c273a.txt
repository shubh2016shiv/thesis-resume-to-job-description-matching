Title: Corporate Service Accountant
Full Description: Elevation Practice are working exclusively with a progressive, leading firm of Chartered Accountants who are looking to appoint a qualified ACA or ACCA practice accountant. Due to extensive growth over the past few years within the firms accounting and advisory service division, the ACA or ACCA qualified accountant will be recruited for the firms Corporate Service Division as Corporate Senior. The successful individual will work on a varied portfolio of clients which will consist of medium to large owner managed business, charities and academies, providing full technical services within financial and statutory accounting and audit. Although the business are looking to recruit a qualified accountant, individuals who have the appropriate level of relevant experience may also be considered and should not be dissuaded from applying (experience to be gained within practice). You will have the ability to plan and execute client audits, supervise junior staff members, along with experience of the preparation of accounts to partner review and preparation of management accountants. Knowledge of the education or charity sector would also be desirable but not a necessity. Due to the exceptional growth that the firm have experienced along with further growth planned, the business can offer an excellent opportunity for individuals looking for further career progression. The firm also offers a friendly and stimulating work environment and actively encourages CPD and will provide inhouse on the job training where needed. Elevation Practice is a specialist division of Elevation Recruitment, focusing on vacancies from part qualified accountants through to Partner and Director positions within Yorkshire and surrounding regions, for further information, please visit our website at www.elevationrecruitment.com for more information.
