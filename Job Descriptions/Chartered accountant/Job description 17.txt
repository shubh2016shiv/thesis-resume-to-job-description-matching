Trainee Accountant (ACCA)- job post
Avonmead Chartered Certified Accountants
Birmingham B4

As a trainee accountant we will support you in your career to become a qualified accountant, recognised by Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA).

An exciting opportunity for a Semi Senior Accountant to join a progressive accountancy firm based in Birmingham city centre.

Key roles and responsibilities include:

Managing a portfolio of clients
Developing a team
Reviewing and preparation of client bookkeeping, VAT returns and payroll
Preparation of year-end accounts for sole traders, partnerships and limited companies
Preparation of management accounts
Filing of statutory accounts with Companies House and HMRC
Supporting with client onboarding
Supporting the partners and directors

Entry requirements
The successful candidate will have at least 2 years of experience working in an accountancy practice and be studying or would like to study towards ACCA qualification. Experience with Xero cloud accounting software is essential.

Excellent communication and numerical skills are vital. The individual will have great attention to detail, be able to work well individually as well as within a team, and have a pro-active approach to work.
