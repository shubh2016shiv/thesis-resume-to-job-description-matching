Title: Personal Tax Assistant
Full Description: This is an excellent opportunity to join the Personal Tax team in one of Yorkshire's leading independent firms of Chartered Accountants and Business Advisors. The role will be client facing and the successful candidate will be responsible for handling a portfolio of personal tax clients and building/developing client relationships. Primarily, the individual will be responsible for completing and reviewing Self Assessment tax returns, and assisting the team in both compliance and planning issues. The practice is looking for a confident, selfmotivated team player with good interpersonal skills. Prior experience of Self Assessment and Personal Tax in a practice environment is essential and experience of managing a client portfolio and client facing is desirable. Qualification to at least ATT level is desirable but not essential if personal tax experience is good.
