Title: Group Financial Accountant
Full Description: Our client a highly successful and growing software group is looking to recruit a credible and experienced Group Financial Accountant into their head office team. Responsibilities will include: preparation of the monthly consolidated financial accounts, annual accounts for group under IFRS, act as primary contact for external audit, manage improvements to the systems and procedures, help with developing junior team member and various ad hoc projects as required. The successful candidate will ideally be a will be a qualified chartered accountant (ideally out of one of the larger firms) with up to date technical skills, an eye for detail and the ability to communicate effectively at all levels. Strong IT skills and the drive to succeed are also required. This is an excellent entry point into a challenging organisation with opportunities to develop and progress.
