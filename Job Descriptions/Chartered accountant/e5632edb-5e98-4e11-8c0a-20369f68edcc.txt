Title: Part Qualified Accountant
Full Description: Grafton Banks Finance is delighted to be working with a long established and highly regarded firm of Chartered Accountants in the Crawley area, West Sussex to recruit a Part Qualified Accountant to join their busy team. Our client is a friendly and successful firm who are recruiting due to an increase in the volume of business. They work with a wide range of clients across the South East and the role will be a mix of auditing and accounts preparation and corporate tax work. You will gain excellent exposure to all areas of general practice and receive excellent training and support as you progress your career in accountancy practice. This is a great opportunity if you already have at least a couple of years practice experience, ideally including audit, are working towards ACCA or ACA and are now looking to expand your experience in a new firm. The firm offer a full study package as well as an attractive basic salary and benefits.
