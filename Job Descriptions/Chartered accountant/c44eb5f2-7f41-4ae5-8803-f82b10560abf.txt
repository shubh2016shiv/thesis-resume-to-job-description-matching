Title: AAT Accounts Senior  leading Chartered Accountants
Full Description: Due to growth within the practices client base, a new role has been created for an experienced Senior Accountant to join this leading independent firm of Chartered Accountants in Bradford. Working in a well established, friendly and supportive team you will provide a full range of accountancy services to SME clients ranging up to 8m t/o. This will included preparation review of statutory management accounts, computation of clients tax liabilities in both corporate, business and personal front, vat bookkeeping and ad hoc client advisory services. Some exposure to audit may also be included, however this can be tailored to the successful candidates preference and experience. To succeed in this role you will either be AAT qualified or qualified by experience and have a minimum of 5 years experience working in an accountancy practice. Randstad Financial Professional encourage applications from individuals of all ages backgrounds. Appointment will be made on merit alone but candidates must be able to demonstrate their ability to work in the UK.
