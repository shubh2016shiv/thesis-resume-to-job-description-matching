Title: Audit Manager
Full Description: Our client, a growing firm of Chartered Accountants based in Essex has an opportunity for an Audit Manager to join them. The Audit Manager will take responsibility for a portfolio of clients ensuring high quality service delivery. The role will involve; overseeing the audit process, leading the fieldwork process for larger audits, preparing and signing off budget with clients, meeting with clients pre and post audit to discuss and resolve queries, delegating work to supervisors, seniors, semi seniors and juniors. Furthermore, other duties involve; reviewing and preparing financial statements, assisting with various internal projects to develop and improve processes and procedures, various adhoc duties. The successful candidate will be ACA/ACCA qualified with at least 5 years postqualified experience and proven experience of leading audit teams. The candidate must have an indepth technical knowledge of audit requirements and excellent client facing skills. In return, the candidate will receive a competitive salary and benefits along with excellent prospects for future progression.
