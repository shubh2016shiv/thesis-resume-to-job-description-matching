Title: Senior Financial Accounting Manager
Full Description: A Senior Financial Accounting Manager is sought by one of the world's premier branded leisure companies; they have a suite of major brands and are a true industry leader with a multibillion turnover. They enjoy a presence in some of the most sought after locations in the UK and across the globe and have a name that is synonymous with both luxury and value. Reporting to the Financial Accounting Director you will be responsible for leading a team of 4 people and have overall responsibility for the following:  Consolidation and reporting process for Balance sheets across the European and International businesses  Provision of financial information to the Treasury and Tax Teams  Foreign exchange analysis  Preparation of statutory accounts  Internal Control process and SOX compliance  Ad hoc support to the Financial reporting team during busy periods You will ensure that all submissions received from subsidiaries & OpCo's across the world are consolidated on an accurate and timely fashion and that process improvements are constantly sought. You will also ensure that your team is motivated and developed. The ideal candidate will be a graduate, qualified Chartered Accountant (ACA / CA) from either practice or industry. You will have strong technical experience of IFRS (and ideally some US GAAP) and will demonstrate both cultural awareness and real people management / development skills. Further, you will have an analytical mindset and will enjoy working with large amounts of data.
