Title: Client Portfolio Manager
Full Description: A well established firm of Chartered Accountants are recruiting for a Client Portfolio Manager vacancy based in Nottingham. The purpose of the role is to deliver quality services in a timely basis to a range of clients. Reporting directly into partner level and you will be responsible for the preparation of assignments for partner review such as accounts, tax, superannuation, reviews, benchmarking and briefing notes. You will also have some staff management responsibility. you will be ACA/ACCA qualified or finalist with previous experience as a portfolio holder.
