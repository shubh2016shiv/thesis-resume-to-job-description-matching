Title: Audit & Accounts SemiSenior
Full Description: My client, a leading firm of Chartered Accountants with offices across Yorkshire, is looking for an Accounts and Audit SemiSenior to join the expanding team in their Leeds office. The role will involve the preparation of accounts for a variety of clients, general tax return work and assisting with audits. In return the firm will offer full ACCA study support as well as inhouse training. The ideal candidate will be AAT qualified with previous experience of working in General Practice. Audit experience is not essential and this gives the successful candidate a great opportunity to gain wider experience and progress your career to the next level.
