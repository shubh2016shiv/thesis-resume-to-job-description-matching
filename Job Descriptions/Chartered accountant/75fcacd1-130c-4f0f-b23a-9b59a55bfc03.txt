Title: Financial Reporting Analyst
Full Description: Financial Reporting Analyst required for an international FTSE listed leisure business based in Central London. In this key role you will be responsible for the reporting and analysis for a region. This will involve preparing monthly analysis, explaining key trends and variances which will be presented to Senior Directors and the PLC board. You will also take a key role in preparing the group consolidation for the monthly reporting, budgeting and forecasting. You will be qualified chartered accountant who has had some experience outside of practice. The ideal candidate will also have the ability and desire to take responsibility for driving process improvements within group reporting and analysis. Delivery within this role will then enable you to rapidly accelerate your career in this exciting consumer business.
