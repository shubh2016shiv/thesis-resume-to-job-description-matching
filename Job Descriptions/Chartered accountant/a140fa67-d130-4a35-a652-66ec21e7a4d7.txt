Title: Audit and Accounts Senior
Full Description: A highly successful firm of chartered accountants based in Swindon, with an excellent reputation, undergoing continued growth and expansion. The firm and culture offer genuine career progression and development prospects for individuals looking to develop further skills and gain greater responsibility and develop their careers. Joining as an Audit and Accounts Senior, based at the firms offices in Swindon, you will be responsible for auditing accounts, interpreting and verifying information provided by clients, preparing annual and management accounts, completing tax computations and drafting letters, emails and other documents for clients, the tax office and registrar of companies. You will work on a wide range of clients, across varied industries and turnovers with involvement in added value project work and within a culture and firm where you will be encouraged to develop further skills and progress in your career. Genuine opportunities for long term career development are on offer for candidates looking to join an expanding and highly successful firm of chartered accountants. The firm, partners and senior managers are looking for a part qualified, finalist ACA / ACCA, or qualified by experience audit and accounts senior with demonstrable experience of leading small, medium or large audits and providing support to junior staff.
