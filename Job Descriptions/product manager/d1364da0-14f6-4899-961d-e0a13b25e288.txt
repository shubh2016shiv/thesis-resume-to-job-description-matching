Title: Product Manager
Full Description: If you are looking for real variety in your next campaign management role, this lovely Product Manager role is for you. Working across press, inserts, door drops, DRTV, mail, email and ppc, you'll liaise with external suppliers and internal colleagues to deliver effective campaigns. You will need excellent campaign management skills, managing the whole process from start to finish, with experience briefing agencies and print. You ll need to be analytical as well as creative looking at responses and profitability. It s a great opportunity if you want to get close to the product.
