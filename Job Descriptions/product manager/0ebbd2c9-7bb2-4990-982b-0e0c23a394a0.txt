Title: Assistant Product Manager
Full Description: An Assistant Product Manager is required to join a market leading specialist business. Reporting into the Senior product Manager, you will play a key role in defining the product strategy, pricing, market research, competitor analysis and supporting the sales team with product issues. To be considered for this role you will have a solid background in product lead marketing, you will have an ability to use your own initiative and be a self starter. The successful candidate will have the capacity to understand complex products and have an interest in how things are made. My client are a medium sized business based in the Warwickshire area. They are currently unrivalled in their market and were founded over a 125 years ago. They are currently in a period of rapid expansion and are looking for a high calibre individual to join their already strong marketing team.Only applicants with a suitable amount of UK based experience and who have a legal right to work within the UK will be considered.
