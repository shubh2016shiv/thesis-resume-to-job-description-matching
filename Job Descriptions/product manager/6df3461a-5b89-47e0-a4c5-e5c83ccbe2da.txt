Title: Project Manager
Full Description: This highly successful organisation is looking for a confident and professional Marketing professional to join their growing team. Ideally degree or CIM educated, you are high organised and have worked within a project management role previously preferably within a technology based B2B organisation. It is essential that candidates come from a commercially driven technology environment for this role, people who have been involved with physical technology products will also be favoured. Reporting to the global marketing management, you will be charged with liaising with the Campaign team and product managers to formulate progressive marketing plans for each of the established product lines. One key aspect of this position will be working with external partners to develop company collateral in support of the sales function. You will also need good budget management for this role as you'll be juggling multiple projects at one given time. This is a great opportunity for an Online Marketing Executive to join a global company that is part of a group which has grown strongly in recent years through pioneering innovations which are continually being developed. Due to the location of this Cambridgeshire office, this position is commutable from Cambridge, Huntingdon, Bedford, St. Ives, Peterborough, March, Ely, Kettering and St Neots.
