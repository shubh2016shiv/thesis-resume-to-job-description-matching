Title: Product Manager
Full Description: My client produces a range of food to go products and side dishes supplying into major retailers. They are one of the UKs top Food Manufactures and have production sites worldwide. They are currently recruiting for a Product Manager On a day to day basis you will be required to: Innovate new products and processes in response to customer briefs to maintain and grow a creative catalogue of products for the customers To motivate and inspire direct reports up to a managerial level to achieve personal development and continual improvement. Manage focused development of new products to deliver the required margin for their customers and the business. Understand the wants and needs of both internal and external customers Present new product and process facts and ideas clearly, accurately and persuasively to achieve effective day to day running of the department Overall awareness of comparative products from all major retailers/competitors Working to a critical path plan, organise and control resources to deliver products and processes to an agreed quality and timescale. Monitor and benchmark product quality of new and existing products for the customers they are responsible for. Candidates must have experience in a similar role within the food industry, ideally with a food related degree level qualification. Disclaimer: This vacancy is based in the United Kingdom.
