Title: Marketing Manager
Full Description: An exciting opportunity has arisen within a young but growing division of a wellknown Staffordshire based company, that will ideally suit an experienced B2B marketer who is looking for their next challenge. Reporting into the Product Manager and with responsibility for the management of their own budget, the successful candidate will take charge of the strategic planning, development and delivery of marketing communications plans and the analysis and feedback of their success. The Marketing Manager will also be responsible for monitoring and reacting to competitor activity, working with the product department to identify and develop competitive tactical marketing opportunities to support the marketing communications plan, and for ensuring that any opportunities to crosssell across divisions are fully maximised from a marketing and exhibitions perspective. In order to apply for this position you must be flexible, adaptable, tenacious and actionorientated with the ability to work well on your own initiative in a fast paced and changeable environment. You must have broad and recent experience across the full marcomms mix within a B2B role, with strong budget management experience. Superb communication skills will be integral to the success of this role, as will a strong sense of urgency and an ability to react quickly to promotional opportunities or changes in the marketplace.
