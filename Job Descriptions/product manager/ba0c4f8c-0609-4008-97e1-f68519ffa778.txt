Title: Product Manager
Full Description: An accomplished marketer with solid product management experience, it is likely that you are of graduate calibre and working towards or already hold a CIM qualification. You will have experience of successfully working with a portfolio of products and have a clear understanding of the reasons behind their performance, and crucially how to react. You can demonstrate the ability of conveying a product's benefits to the market as well as a track record of launching products with a sales force. As Product Manager, you will support the management of an assigned product portfolio and introduce new products and programmes into the relevant platform. You will drive improvements in sales and profitability through innovating and controlling the portfolio. This role represents a fantastic opportunity for an experienced Product Manager to assume responsibility for a high profile portfolio within a wellrespected and evolving global company. Due to the size of the organisation and their exciting development plans, there are great career prospects on offer along with the superb package. Due to the location of the Northamptonshire office, this role could be of interest to those living in Milton Keynes, Rugby, Bedford, Northampton, Corby, Leicester and Market Harborough.
