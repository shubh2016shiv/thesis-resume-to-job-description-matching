Title: Product Manager
Full Description: Our client, an up market tour operator, established for over 25 years are now looking for a Product Manager to join their vibrant team. The ideal candidate shall be dealing with contract negotiations with existing potential suppliers, monitor performance of all product lines, secure special/promotional product offers and attend travel shows. In conjunction with marketing, the ideal candidate shall be producing brochures and dealing with pricing and website content. You will manage our client existing product including Norway, Italy, Morocco China along with developing new products. You must demonstrate the ability to problem solve, possess resilience, determination and possess excellent attention to detail. To be considered for this exciting opportunity you must have product development and management experience within the travel industry and have strong interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to develop relationships at all levels. In return, our client will offer a great salary, bonus and the opportunity to travel to some exciting destinations.
