Title: Product Manager
Full Description: Exciting expanding Christchurch Company requires a new Product Manager who will be involved in sourcing and developing new products. The Product Manager will be communicating with the Far East, spotting new product opportunities and presenting them to the sales team and briefing on packaging. The ideal Product Manager must be commercially aware, highly professional and a desire to do well and grow with the Company. Experience in product management would be desirable however the company will train for the right candidate
