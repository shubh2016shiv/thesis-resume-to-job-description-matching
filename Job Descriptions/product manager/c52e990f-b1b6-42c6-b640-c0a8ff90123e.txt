Title: Brand / Product Manager
Full Description: Our client is a leading global consumer business based in Greater Manchester and they are now seeking a Brand/Product Manager to develop and deliver the NPD pipeline. As Brand/Product Manager, you will report into the UK Marketing Director and be responsible for unlocking insights and identifying business opportunities. You will lead the innovation process making recommendations on product assortment and pricing. You will own the NPD from ideation, through to the planning stages and subsequent launch. You will have a background in product marketing/product development within consumer durables, or perhaps be a FMCG marketer looking for a change in sector. Whichever your background, it is important that you are experienced in managing the innovation process end to end. Solid commercial acumen is essential and this is a pivotal role requiring someone with gravitas, presence and an entrepreneurial flair.
