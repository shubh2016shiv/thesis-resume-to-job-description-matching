Title: Assistant Product Manager
Full Description: Excellent opportunity for a junior marketing individual to join one ofYorkshire's leading consumer businesses as an assistant product manager. With a number of leading Brands and Products across multiple categories they are seen as a major force on the high street Reporting into a Product Manager the Assistant Product Manager will work closely with a key retail customer and propose commercial ideas for new product ranges to meet their requirements. The role will involve monitoring and analysis of marketplace activity and sales and research data to develop your customer plan. You will assist with the product development process from concept to delivery and assist in the preparation for buyer presentations. The ideal candidate will be a marketing graduate with a years marketing experience either as a placement year or post university, you will possess excellent communication and analytical skills, be commercially focused and have strong attention to detail.
