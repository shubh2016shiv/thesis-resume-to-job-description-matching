Title: Product Manager
Full Description: Product Manager required to support existing business and help grow sales of a select range of specialist medical products to the NHS and private hospitals throughout the UK and Ireland. Products: Dermatology, intensive care, orthopaedics, theatre supplies, rehabilitation, orthotics. Job brief: Liaison with suppliers Technical support Product training Coordinate product marketing Monitor and report on changes in the UK medical market Experience: Clinical and / or medical sales experience Educated to degree level Good communication skills UK driving licence Other details: The role does involve some travelling but is mostly office based (in the Forest of Dean). The role reports directly to the Managing Director. Candidates have the option to use video conferencing (Skype) for the first interview if more convenient.
